About Us

Who We Are?

Cirrusflink is a technology-driven company that helps businesses modernize, manage, and secure their IT environments, both on-premise and in the cloud. We offer a multidisciplinary range of services, including Cybersecurity (audits, VAPT, XDR, SOC), Compliance & Certifications (ISO27001, HIPAA, GDPR, SOC 2, PCI DSS), and Business Intelligence through AI-driven data engineering and advanced analytics. Our expertise also extends to Cloud solutions (sovereign, edge, private, and public), Datacentre Modernization (HCI, SDI, Backup, DR & BC), and Network Intelligence with structured cabling, secure gateways, and next-gen firewalls.

Driven by innovation and deep industry insight, we deliver tailored solutions to key sectors like manufacturing, banking, healthcare, and education. Our skilled teams combine cutting-edge tools and technical expertise to generate measurable outcomes, helping you transform legacy datacenters and manage sophisticated cybersecurity threats at every stage of your digital evolution.
